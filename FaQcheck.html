SunOfTheSoil






SunOfTheSoil Cocktails is a shared database of cocktail recipes.
After you sign up, you can build a Cocktail Book composed of your own recipes
and/or those that others have shared. Casual users may search for and use recipes
without creating an account.

Sponsors and advertisers pay to place their advertisements before you.
Because SunOfTheSoil Cocktails knows what the ingredients are, it can do a good job of placing,
advertisements that are likely to be interesting. In the future, there may be some paid
premium services for user and retailers.

If you share our love of craft cocktails and you ... are exploring the craft cocktail world and are looking for high-quality cocktail recipes,
are a cocktail enthusiast with an existing collection of cocktails, or
are a cocktail professional looking for inspiration and a strong organizational tool ...

Yes. At some point there may be a premium account with advanced features,
but a basic account will remain free.
